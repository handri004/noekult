About Footprint:

At the core of our endeavor lies a comprehensive exploration
presented in a meticulously crafted paper. Delving deep into the
realms of carbon emissions and green digitalization, this paper serves
as the foundation for our initiatives. Building upon its findings, we proudly
introduce our latest innovation, a cutting-edge carbon emissions app. Designed
to empower users, our app revolutionizes the way individuals engage with their environmental
footprint. By seamlessly integrating variables such as transportation mode and distance, users can effortlessly
calculate their carbon emissions. Through this transformative tool, we aim to inspire mindful choices and foster a sustainable future.
